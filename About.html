Hi! I'm Dhikshitha, a second-year college student at SRM Ramapuram. This is my very first web development project — a Restaurant Menu Web Page titled "Global Bites". I created it out of curiosity and excitement to explore the world of front-end development using HTML and CSS.


The name "Global Bites" reflects the variety of cuisines featured in the menu — a celebration of flavors from around the world. Through this project, I learned how to design engaging layouts, style pages with CSS, and bring a real-world concept to life with visuals and interactivity.


Before this, I completed my schooling at Velammal Vidhyashram and studied two backend technologies: Python and MySQL. Now, having explored front-end development, I’m excited to continue learning and building more interactive web applications. Thank you for visiting my first project — I hope you enjoy browsing Global Bites!
